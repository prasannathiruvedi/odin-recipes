Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, olives, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella is common)
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake in the oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!
